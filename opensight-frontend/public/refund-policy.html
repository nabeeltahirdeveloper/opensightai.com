Refund Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

This policy explains how refunds are handled for digital products and subscription-based services.

1. Digital Products & Online Services

Because our service is a digital product that is accessible immediately, refunds cannot be issued once the service has been accessed or used.

2. 14-Day Refund Eligibility (Bank Requirement)

You may request a refund within 14 days of purchase only if you have not accessed or used the service.

3. Subscription Renewals

Subscription payments are non-refundable after they are processed.

You may cancel future renewals at any time.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email: suuport@OpenSightai.com

Include:

  • Order number
  • Email used for purchase
  • Reason for request (optional)

Refunds are processed within 7–14 business days.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted if:

  • The service was accessed or used
  • You changed your mind after using the service
  • Renewal payment already processed
  • You failed to cancel before the renewal date
  • System incompatibility due to customer device limitations
  • Violation of Terms of Service

6. Defective Service or Technical Issues

If the service is inaccessible due to a system error, we will:

  • Fix the issue,
  • Provide access, or
  • Issue a refund when appropriate.

7. Chargebacks (Banks Require This Section)

Before filing a chargeback, customers must contact our support team.

We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly.

If the dispute remains unresolved, customers may follow their card issuer's dispute process.
